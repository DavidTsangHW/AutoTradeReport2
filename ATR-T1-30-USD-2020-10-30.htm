About this project

In the past, most retail investors primarily rely on major news sources or analysts for making trading decisions.

Those days were long gone since fake news and rumours fly all over the world, trustworthiness and truthfulness of news is now becoming an issue, data has become the most reliable partner.

This program is trained with 20 years of data, and started the first estimate on 22 Jan 2020, analysing ~500 stock symbols trading in Australia and United States

It also enables retail investors to explore stock markets worldwide with ease and confidence.

The trading strategy is to capture volatility as many as possible. The longest holding period is 365 days; the estimated profit% is at least 30%; Sell when estimated price or date reached, whichever comes first.

I use GitHub to timestamp every estimate as Proof of Existence. All archives since 22 Jan 2020 is available here.

This page is updated daily.
